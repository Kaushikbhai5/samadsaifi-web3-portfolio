About Me

I got into crypto back in 2018 after stumbling upon a YouTube video about airdrops. What started as curiosity quickly turned into a passion for Web3, DeFi, and community building. Over the years, I’ve worked with projects like Galxe, Web3Go, Nolus Protocol, Mantis, Astra Nova, and Zora, helping them grow and engage their communities.

I specialize in community management, content creation, and campaign activations—whether it’s running AMAs, Learn & Earn campaigns, or ambassador programs, I focus on making communities more interactive and valuable for users. I also have experience setting up and managing Discord bots, which helps automate moderation, engagement, and security.

For me, Web3 isn’t just about technology—it’s about people, decentralization, and creating a space where everyone can contribute and grow. I’m always looking for new challenges and ways to bring value to the space.
